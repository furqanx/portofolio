Big Data Challenge: Multi-class Text Classification of Tweets Related to Elections

Diaz Darsya Rizqullah, Furqan Al Ghifari Zulva, Muhammad Farid

May, 2024 - June, 2024

Github Link

Developed a machine learning model for classifying election-related tweets into 8 categories, collaborating in a team. Leveraged a fine-tuned IndoBERT model, focusing on data preprocessing, model training, and evaluation. The model achieved high accuracy and led the team to the semifinals, placing 39th out of over 400 teams in the Satria Data 2024 competition.
